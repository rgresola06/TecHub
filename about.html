About Us

At TecHub, our core principle has always been to offer and sell ONLY all-original games and software to our customers. Even though piracy and pirated products proliferate in the market and selling only all-original games and software will mean competing in an unequal playing field, we have remained steadfast and shall always remain steadfast in standing by our core principles as we take the trust and confidence of our customers with utmost seriousness and importance.
